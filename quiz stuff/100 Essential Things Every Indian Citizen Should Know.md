# 100 Essential Things Every Indian Citizen Should Know: A Quiz

This quiz is designed to test and educate on key aspects of Indian citizenship, covering the Constitution, laws, history, geography, government, national symbols, culture, and economy. Each question is followed by its answer for easy learning. Questions are grouped by category for better organization.

## Section 1: Indian Constitution and Fundamental Rights (Questions 1-15)

1. **What are the six Fundamental Rights guaranteed by the Indian Constitution?**  
   Answer: Right to Equality, Right to Freedom, Right against Exploitation, Right to Freedom of Religion, Cultural and Educational Rights, and Right to Constitutional Remedies.

2. **Under which Article is the Right to Equality provided?**  
   Answer: Articles 14–18.

3. **What does the Right to Freedom include?**  
   Answer: Freedom of speech and expression, assembly, association, movement, residence, and profession (Articles 19–22).

4. **Which Fundamental Right prohibits child labor and human trafficking?**  
   Answer: Right against Exploitation (Articles 23–24).

5. **What is the Right to Freedom of Religion?**  
   Answer: Freedom to profess, practice, and propagate religion (Articles 25–28).

6. **Which Article protects the rights of minorities?**  
   Answer: Cultural and Educational Rights (Articles 29–30).

7. **What is the remedy for violation of Fundamental Rights?**  
   Answer: Right to Constitutional Remedies (Article 32).

8. **How many Fundamental Duties are there in the Indian Constitution?**  
   Answer: 11 (Article 51A).

9. **Name one Fundamental Duty.**  
   Answer: To abide by the Constitution and respect its ideals and institutions, the National Flag, and the National Anthem.

10. **When were Fundamental Duties added to the Constitution?**  
    Answer: By the 42nd Amendment in 1976.

11. **What is the Preamble of the Indian Constitution?**  
    Answer: It declares India as a sovereign, socialist, secular, democratic republic.

12. **Who is the guardian of the Constitution?**  
    Answer: The Supreme Court of India.

13. **What is Article 21?**  
    Answer: Right to Life and Personal Liberty.

14. **Can Fundamental Rights be suspended?**  
    Answer: Yes, during a national emergency, except Articles 20 and 21.

15. **What was removed from Fundamental Rights in 1978?**  
    Answer: Right to Property (by the 44th Amendment).

## Section 2: Important Laws and Rights (Questions 16-30)

16. **Under which law can a woman not be arrested after sunset or before sunrise?**  
    Answer: Criminal Procedure Code, Section 46.

17. **What is the legal blood alcohol limit for driving?**  
    Answer: 30mg per 100ml of blood (Motor Vehicle Act 1988, Section 185).

18. **Can a police officer refuse to file an FIR?**  
    Answer: No, punishable under IPC 166A.

19. **What is the maximum number of riders on a two-wheeler?**  
    Answer: Two (Motor Vehicle Act 1988, Section 128).

20. **Is helmet mandatory for two-wheeler riders?**  
    Answer: Yes (Motor Vehicle Act 1988, Section 129).

21. **What right allows free use of hotel washrooms?**  
    Answer: Indian Sarais Act 1887.

22. **Can unmarried couples stay in hotels?**  
    Answer: Yes, if both are adults.

23. **What compensation is available for gas cylinder accidents?**  
    Answer: Up to Rs. 50 lakh (Explosives Act 1884).

24. **Is live-in relationship legal?**  
    Answer: Yes, recognized as similar to marriage.

25. **What is the Right to Information?**  
    Answer: Allows citizens to seek information from public authorities (RTI Act 2005).

26. **What is the minimum marriage age for women?**  
    Answer: 18 years (Prohibition of Child Marriage Act 2006).

27. **Can divorce be filed within one year of marriage?**  
    Answer: No, except in exceptional cases.

28. **What is the Right to Education?**  
    Answer: Free and compulsory education for children aged 6-14 (Article 21A).

29. **Is PDA illegal?**  
    Answer: No, but obscene acts in public are punishable (IPC Section 294).

30. **What is the legal drinking age in Delhi?**  
    Answer: 25 years.

## Section 3: Key Historical Events (Questions 31-45)

    Answer: August 15, 1947.

    Answer: Division into India and Pakistan in 1947.

33. **Who assassinated Mahatma Gandhi?**  
    Answer: Nathuram Godse, on January 30, 1948.

34. **When did India become a republic?**  
    Answer: January 26, 1950.

35. **What was the first Indo-Pak war?**  
    Answer: Over Kashmir in 1947-48.

36. **Who was India's first Prime Minister?**  
    Answer: Jawaharlal Nehru.

37. **What event led to the creation of Bangladesh?**  
    Answer: Indo-Pak War of 1971.

38. **When was the Emergency declared?**  
    Answer: June 25, 1975, by Indira Gandhi.

39. **Who was assassinated in 1984?**  
    Answer: Indira Gandhi.

40. **What was the Babri Masjid demolition?**  
    Answer: In 1992, leading to communal riots.

41. **When did India conduct nuclear tests?**  
    Answer: Pokhran-II in 1998.

42. **What was the Kargil War?**  
    Answer: Conflict with Pakistan in 1999.

43. **When was the Right to Information Act passed?**  
    Answer: 2005.

44. **What is the significance of 2014?**  
    Answer: Narendra Modi became Prime Minister, marking BJP's rise.

45. **When was Article 370 abrogated?**  
    Answer: August 5, 2019.

59. **What is India's population rank?**  
    Answer: 1st (over 1.4 billion).

60. **Which river originates in Tibet?**

## Section 5: Government Structure (Questions 61-70)

61. **What type of government is India?**  
    Answer: Federal parliamentary republic.

62. **Who is the head of state?**  
    Answer: President.

63. **Who is the head of government?**  
    Answer: Prime Minister.

64. **What are the two houses of Parliament?**  
    Answer: Lok Sabha (House of the People) and Rajya Sabha (Council of States).

65. **Who appoints the Prime Minister?**  
    Answer: President.

66. **What is the role of the Supreme Court?**  
    Answer: Judicial review and guardian of the Constitution.

67. **How many tiers of government in India?**  
    Answer: Three: Union, State, and Local.

68. **Who heads the state executive?**  
    Answer: Governor (appointed by President).

69. **What is the Union List?**  
    Answer: Subjects on which only the Union can legislate (Seventh Schedule).

70. **Who is the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces?**  
    Answer: President.

## Section 8: Economic Basics (Questions 91-100)

91. **What is India's GDP rank by nominal value?**  
    Answer: 4th largest.

92. **What is GDP?**  
    Answer: Total value of goods and services produced in a country.

93. **What is India's main economic sector?**  
    Answer: Services (about 55% of GDP).

94. **What is inflation?**  
    Answer: Rise in general price levels.

95. **Who manages monetary policy in India?**  
    Answer: Reserve Bank of India (RBI).

96. **What is fiscal policy?**  
    Answer: Government spending and taxation to influence the economy.

97. **What is India's currency?**  
    Answer: Indian Rupee (INR).

98. **What is the poverty line in India?**  
    Answer: About 23.9% below poverty line (2022).

99. **What is India's unemployment rate?**  
    Answer: Around 4.9% (2025 est.).

100. **What is India's major export?**  
     Answer: Refined petroleum products.
