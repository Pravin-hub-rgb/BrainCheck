### Sub-section 4.2: Coastline, Seas & Islands (Questions 11-15)

11. **What is India's total coastline length as per the latest 2025 reassessment?**  
    Answer: 11,098.81 km (increased from previous 7,516.6 km due to high-resolution GIS mapping and inclusion of intricate details/islands).

12. **Which state has the longest coastline in India?**  
    Answer: Gujarat (2,340.62 km as per latest data).

13. **Which entity (state/UT) has the overall longest coastline?**  
    Answer: Andaman & Nicobar Islands (~3,083.50 km).

14. **Which seas border India on the west and east?**  
    Answer: Arabian Sea (west), Bay of Bengal (east), and Indian Ocean (south).

15. **What is the southernmost point of India?**  
    Answer: Indira Point (Pygmalion Point) in Great Nicobar Island (Andaman & Nicobar Islands).
