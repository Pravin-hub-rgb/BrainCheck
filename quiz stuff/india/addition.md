### Additions to Section 5: Government Structure & Polity (now +5 questions, total ~35)

31. **What is the maximum strength of Rajya Sabha as per the Constitution?**  
    Answer: 250 members (233 elected + 12 nominated by President; current strength 245).

32. **Who has the power to dissolve the Lok Sabha?**  
    Answer: The President, on the advice of the Prime Minister (or in cases of no-confidence/no majority government).

33. **What does CAG stand for, and what is its role?**  
    Answer: Comptroller and Auditor General of India (Article 148); independent constitutional body that audits Union and State government accounts, reports to Parliament/President.

34. **How many tiers are there in the Panchayati Raj system?**  
    Answer: Three tiers: Gram Panchayat (village level), Panchayat Samiti/Block Panchayat (intermediate/block level), Zila Parishad (district level).

35. **How many members are there in the Election Commission of India?**  
    Answer: Usually 3: 1 Chief Election Commissioner + 2 Election Commissioners (multi-member since 1993; can vary but fixed at 3 currently).
